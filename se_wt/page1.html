What is Expense Splitter?

We are developing a smart web-based application to automate and simplify the management of shared expenses among groups — be it friends, roommates, or project teams. Currently, people rely on informal methods which are error-prone and create misunderstandings. Our system provides a platform where users can create expense groups, add shared expenses, and automatically calculate who owes whom.

01. Smart Automation

Auto-calculates individual balances with algorithmic precision, removing the need for manual arithmetic.

02. Group Management

Create and manage distinct groups for trips, flatmates, or events with full member tracking.

03. Full Transparency

Settlement history ensures complete financial clarity and transaction tracking among all users.
