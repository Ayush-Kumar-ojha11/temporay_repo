Motivation & Objectives

The Problem Statement

Shared expenses naturally degrade into financial confusion and interpersonal friction.

01. High Manual Effort

Groups rely on spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, or memory — all highly time-consuming and error-prone methods for tracking shared costs.

02. Frequent Calculation Errors

Manual arithmetic inevitably leads to mistakes in splitting bills, causing under or over-payment and repeated disputes among members.

03. No Transparency

Existing informal methods lack full financial history. Members cannot easily verify who paid what and who owes whom.

04. Lack of Settlement Tracking

There is no mechanism to mark debts as settled, leading to old, unpaid amounts lingering indefinitely without resolution.

05. Privacy & Security Gaps

Sharing financial data through messaging apps exposes sensitive information with no encryption or access control.

Project Objectives

01

Web Application

Web application engineered to manage shared expenses efficiently.

02

Authentication

Allow users to securely register and log in using email/password with encrypted credential management.

03

Group Management

Enable users to create expense groups, add members, and record shared expense events with descriptions.

04

Algorithmic Calculation

Automatically compute exact individual balances — who owes whom and how much — with zero manual arithmetic.

05

Debt Settlement

Allow users to mark payments as settled and maintain a clear record of all transactions.

06

History Tracking

Maintain a complete history of all group expenses and settlements for full financial transparency.

07

Intuitive Interface

Provide a highly user-friendly interface prioritising clear data entry, readable balances, and minimal friction.

08

Secure Architecture

Build a secure web app using encryption to protect user transaction data.
