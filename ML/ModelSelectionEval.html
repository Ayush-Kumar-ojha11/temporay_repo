Pipeline Highlights

The final pipeline utilizes Stratified K-Fold cross-validation with exactly 5 folds to ensure that every variation of stress levels is perfectly represented during training. The predictions from our baseline and neural network models are then fed directly into an XGBoost meta-learner (Stacking) to correct individual model biases and produce a highly accurate final score.

Performance Evaluation

To truly understand how well our model distinguishes between high-stress and low-stress individuals, we plotted the Receiver Operating Characteristic (ROC) and Precision-Recall (POC) curves.

The ROC curve demonstrates an exceptional Area Under the Curve (AUC) of 0.98, while the Precision-Recall curve maintains an AUC of 0.90, proving the model's high reliability and precision in identifying elevated stress risk across the dataset.
