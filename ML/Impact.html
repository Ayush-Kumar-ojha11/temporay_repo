Making A Difference

Technology is only as good as the lives it improves. Sleep Sense was explicitly designed to align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to provide actionable, accessible health insights.

The Reality of Stress

We are democratizing mental health insights. By predicting stress through everyday smartphone habits, we eliminate the need for expensive clinical sleep studies for underserved populations.

Rest & Recovery

Sleep Sense provides the underlying architecture for intelligent wellness apps, giving individuals and companies privacy-first tools to monitor burnout and encourage healthier digital boundaries.

SDG Target 3.4

Goal 3: Good Health & Well-being

"Promote mental health and well-being and reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases."

Our Mapping: By quantifying stress risks directly from daily digital habits, Sleep Sense supports the early detection of harmful sleep deprivation and high-stress patterns long before they escalate into severe clinical issues.
