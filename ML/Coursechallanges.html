Challenge 01Grasping the Basics

The Obstacle: When the course started, Unit 1 was honestly very hard for me to understand. I was still figuring out the fundamental terminology and concepts of the subject.

The Solution: I decided to tackle this by reading the textbook chapter by chapter. It took a lot of time, but eventually, all the theory clicked, and I really started to see the beauty of the subject.

Challenge 02Navigating Trickier Concepts

The Obstacle: Moving into Unit 2, the topics introduced were definitely a bit more tricky and complex than what we covered in Unit 1.

The Solution: Because I had spent so much time thoroughly clearing up my basics in the first unit, this actually wasn't a big deal. Having a strong foundation made it much easier to adapt and understand the harder algorithms.

The Obstacle: Unit 3 was a whole different thing since we started diving into unsupervised learning, like clustering and finding hidden structures.

The Solution: By this point, my core concepts were so clear that I just had to read through the topics once. Concepts like clustering made intuitive sense immediately because of the groundwork I laid earlier.
