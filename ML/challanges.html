Obstacles & Solutions

Data Cleaning Overload

Challenge: Real-world datasets are incredibly messy. Handling missing values, outliers, and inconsistent formats took up the majority of my time.

Solution: I learned to build robust data pipelines using Pandas and Scikit-learn's imputer classes, automating much of the preprocessing workflow.

Model Overfitting

Challenge: My early deep learning models performed perfectly on training data but failed miserably on validation sets.

Solution: Implemented techniques like Dropout, L2 Regularization, and Early Stopping. Understanding the bias-variance tradeoff was key to fixing this.
